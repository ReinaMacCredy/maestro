# Converge Phase

## Purpose
Evaluate and prioritize ideas to select the most promising for action.

## Process

### 1. Define Criteria
Establish evaluation dimensions:

| Criterion | Description | Weight |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Impact | Potential value if implemented | High |
| Feasibility | Technical/resource viability | Medium |
| Novelty | Uniqueness and differentiation | Medium |
| Alignment | Fits with goals/constraints | High |

### 2. Quick Filter
Remove ideas that are:
- ‚ùå Clearly out of scope
- ‚ùå Already implemented
- ‚ùå Technically impossible
- ‚ùå Against core constraints

### 3. Evaluate Remaining Ideas

```markdown
## Evaluation Matrix

| Idea | Impact | Feasibility | Novelty | Alignment | Total |
|------|--------|-------------|---------|-----------|-------|
| [A]  | 3      | 2           | 3       | 3         | 11    |
| [B]  | 2      | 3           | 2       | 3         | 10    |
| [C]  | 3      | 1           | 3       | 2         | 9     |
```
*Scale: 1=Low, 2=Medium, 3=High*

### 4. Select Top Ideas
Choose 3-5 ideas for further development.
