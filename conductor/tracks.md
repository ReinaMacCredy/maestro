## Track Format

When tracks are created, they appear as:

```markdown
## [~] Track: <title>
*ID: <shortname_YYYYMMDD>*
*Link: [spec](conductor/tracks/<id>/spec.md) | [plan](conductor/tracks/<id>/plan.md)*
```

Status markers:
- `[ ]` — Not started
- `[~]` — In progress
- `[x]` — Completed
