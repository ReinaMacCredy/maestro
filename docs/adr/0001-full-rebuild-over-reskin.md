# Full rebuild over agent-surface re-skin

The agent-facing chaos (mission, task, handoff, intake, etc.) reflects internal sprawl across 31 feature directories with no enforced lifecycle. A skills-only re-skin would hide the symptom; the underlying primitives would keep accreting and the chaos would return. We picked a full rebuild to article-shaped primitives, accepting ~3-4 months of churn over weeks-long re-skin, because the goal is positioning maestro as the harness OS, not improving its UX.

Considered alternatives: (A) skills-only re-skin, ~4 weeks but problem returns; (B) re-skin + light internal consolidation, ~6-8 weeks but half-finished. Rejected both.
